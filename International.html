1 Las Vegas

Popular Nightlife

The city of nightlife,entertainment & adventures,It is a seductive mix of decadence, fun and lavish holiday experiences. Tourists love Vegas for its casinos, gaming nights,neon-lit neighbourhoods ,food extravaganzas,lush green parks & quirky museums.

2 Sydney

Massive Darling Harbour

A port city, Sydney is the capital of New South Wales, exuding a truly cosmopolitan vibe. Its known for many sun-kissed beaches, nature parks, vibrant nightlife, adventure experiences and the glittering harbour, encircled by many prominent landmarks.

3 Singapore

Popularly known as Lion City

A melting pot of cultures, Singapore is a cosmopolitan city with a vibrant shopping, food, entertainment and nightlife scene. It is also deep-rooted in history and tradition.

4 Hong Kong

Chinese special administrative region

An ultra-modern city deep rooted in culture, Hong Kong is where swanky skyscrapers stand alongside traditional temples and where shopping, dining and nightlife are some of the biggest draws.
