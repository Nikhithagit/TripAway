    Hill Stations To Visit For A Rejuvenating Trip In Nature's Lap


    Hill Stations - Breathe in the fresh mountain air, treat your eyes to the spectacular views and lose yourself in the charming ambience to feel refreshed from within..

1 Shimla

Hub of Natural Bounties and Winter Sports

The erstwhile British ‘Summer Capital’ is packed with colonial-era architectural marvels, picturesque hilly hotspots, and a toy train tagged as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

2 Kasol

Mini Israel in Himachal Pradesh

The hill-hamlet of Kasol stands at over 1,580 meters above sea level, in Himachal Pradesh, and flaunts raw natural beauty with lush conifer forests, snow-capped Himalayan mountains and winding rivers.

3 Wayanad

Green Paradise in North-East Kerala

Located on the southern tip of the Deccan plateau, Wayanad is home to clear lakes, dense jungles, and deep valleys. This weekend getaway from Bengaluru, is a paradise for trekkers.

4 Leh

White Paradise in Ladakh

Located at a height of 11,500 feet, Leh finds place in the bucket list of many travel enthusiasts. It will leave you in awe with its iconic monasteries & picturesque landscapes..

5 Gulmarg

Heaven in the White Hills of Kashmir

Gulmarg is the crowning glory of Jammu & Kashmir and is located 2,650 meters above sea level. Known as the “Meadow of Flowers”, it boasts of the world’s highest golf-course.

6 Coorg

Scenic Hill station in Banglore

Unwind in the lap of nature in Coorg where aromatic coffee and spice plantations, lush green hills, cascading waterfalls and a distinct old-world charm sooth the senses.
